Travel Tips

Best Time to Book

Book flights 2-3 months in advance for the best deals. Train tickets can be booked up to 6 months ahead.

Airport Transport

Copenhagen Airport is connected to the city center via metro (15 min) and train (12 min).

Travel Documents

EU citizens need only an ID card. Non-EU visitors may require a Schengen visa.

Public Transport

Consider getting a Copenhagen Card for unlimited public transport and free entry to many attractions.

Biking Around

Copenhagen is a bike-friendly city. Rent a bike to explore like a local and enjoy the extensive cycle lanes.

Electricity

Denmark uses European standard Type C and Type K plugs. The voltage is 230V.
